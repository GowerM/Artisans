The History

Artisans has been a proud partner of the Spokane Community since 1987. Our services continually transform to meet the needs of our clients. Artisans began as an agency whose employment offerings centered around on-site woodwork. Over the past 20 years, however, Artisans has grown and undergone many changes.

In 2004, Artisans began to take a new direction that emphasized community-based employment. Now our clients work in community-based jobs providing various services for private and commercial employers. Artisans began providing employment services for less than 10 adults with disabilities, and today provides employment support for more than 70 individuals. Artisans provides job developing and coaching to persons with disabilities for both Individual and Group Supported Employment.

Business partners throughout the community provide work contracts that allow for skill building and assessment. We thank each company for providing work opportunities for persons with disabilities. We have formed long lasting relationships with many companies and individuals in Spokane and continue to build and maintain these relationships.

Our Mission

The Artisans is a non-profit agency providing services necessary for individual job development, employment support and employment opportunities in the community for persons with disabilities.

We are organized to provide the services necessary to each individual to ensure employment goals can be accomplished. We strive to provide stable and supportive work environments for persons with disabilities and for the persons that assist them.
