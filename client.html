Individual Supported Employment (ISE)

Our agency offers a variety of services and support throughout the entire employment process. Our staff works diligently to offer job placements in the community and provides personalized support for each individual, centering efforts on the individual's interests and abilities.

Group Supported Employment (GSE)

Our Group Supported Employment services offer job positions in the community with continuous support provided by staff members. Individuals work in small groups and earn pay based on productivity. Staff members provide training, job development and the supervision necessary to create a constructive work environment.

Community Access

Participants enrolled in this program will receive services focused on activities and goals related to community engagement and integration.

Our Mission

The Artisans is a non-profit agency providing services necessary for individual job development, employment support and employment opportunities in the community for persons with disabilities.

We are organized to provide the services necessary to each individual to ensure employment goals can be accomplished. We strive to provide stable and supportive work environments for persons with disabilities and for the persons that assist them.
